About Dj T Tech

DJ T-TECH, also known as Ruh Bwoi, is a Jamaican music producer, recording artist, and CEO of Fam Pros, Touch Base Productions, and 09Lab. With a passion for trick DJ-ing and a talent for recording, mixing, and mastering exclusive tracks, he has become a prominent figure in the Q9 NY Hip Hop community. His unique style of synthesizer music has earned him recognition as a key player in the industry. Despite growing up in Kingston Jamaica, DJ T-TECH has stayed focused on his goals and has accomplished much in his career.
